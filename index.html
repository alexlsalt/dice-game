Instructions (below)

1. Enter a winning score number into the box (this can be changed while playing the game)
Note: if no number is entered into the score box, the winning score is 100 by default.
2. Roll the die and try to get as many points as possible
3. To count your current score toward your game score, click 'Hold'
4. If you roll a '1,' your current points will be lost and it will be the next player's turn
5. The first player to reach the winning number of points wins
6. Warning! If two 6's are rolled in a row, the active player loses all points
